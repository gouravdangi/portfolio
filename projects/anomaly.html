06 — ML Pipeline

Anomaly Detection via Whisper + LLM

Detects potential mis-selling in sales call recordings using speech-to-text transcription and an LLM compliance analysis pipeline.

The problem

Financial regulators require firms to monitor sales calls for mis-selling — pitching products that don't match a customer's stated risk profile or financial goals. Manually reviewing thousands of calls per week is expensive and inconsistent.

The approach

Recorded calls are transcribed using OpenAI Whisper (self-hosted). The transcript is then passed to an LLM pipeline (via LangChain) that checks for red-flag patterns: promising guaranteed returns, omitting risk disclosures, or misrepresenting fees. Flagged calls are scored and routed to a compliance officer with a structured report.

Output

Each flagged call produces an auto-generated compliance report with a risk score, highlighted transcript segments, and the specific regulatory clause violated. The pipeline surfaces the most critical issues first, allowing compliance teams to focus their attention on the highest-risk interactions.

Visualisation

Plotly dashboards aggregate flag rates by agent, product type, and time period — giving managers a real-time view of compliance risk across the salesforce. Anomaly scores use a z-score-based outlier method over rolling 7-day windows.

Technology stack

Whisper STT Speech Diarization LLM as Judge Python NLP

Why this matters

Mis-selling in financial services carries heavy regulatory penalties (e.g., FCA enforcement actions). Automating first-pass detection dramatically reduces compliance audit costs and gives firms an auditable AI paper trail. The LLM also surfaces training insights — common phrases that correlate with mis-selling risk — feeding back into agent coaching.
