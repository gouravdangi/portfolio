03 — Conversational AI Demo

Agri RAG Chatbot

An intent-aware agricultural advisory chatbot. Ask about crops, pests, fertilisers, irrigation, or soil health — or try a suggestion chip.

The project

Agricultural advisory chatbot built with Google Dialogflow for intent classification and spaCy for named-entity recognition. A RAG knowledge base surfaces contextual answers to farmers' queries in real time.

How it works

The bot classifies the user's intent (crop advice, pest management, fertiliser schedule, etc.) then retrieves the most relevant context from its knowledge base. The demo here implements the same intent-matching logic client-side.

Production impact

At JM Financial, a similar RAG chatbot (using spaCy + intent classification) resolves 86% of preliminary customer-support queries before routing to a human agent.
